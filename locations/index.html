Finding Us

How to Get Here

Fifth Avenue runs off Water Street (OH-44) in downtown Chardon, a block off the square. Most patients find us in under a minute once they're on Fifth.

From OH-44 / Water St

Head into downtown Chardon on Water Street. Turn onto Fifth Avenue — we're in the building with blue accents and a marked Suite #3 entry.

Parking

Free dedicated spots directly in front of our suite. No street parking needed and no parking fees.

Check-in

Come in through the Suite #3 door. No front desk maze — your clinician greets you directly and walks you back.

Accessibility

Ground-floor, single-level space. ADA-accessible entry. No stairs to navigate.
