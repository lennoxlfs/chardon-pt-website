About

Physical Therapy Built Around You

Chardon Performance Therapy was founded in 2020 with a single belief: patients deserve better than the assembly-line PT model. Every session is 1-on-1, every plan is personal, and every outcome is built on your goals — not ours.

Our Story

Why We Built This Practice

Before opening Chardon Performance Therapy, Dave Nelson spent years in the traditional PT model — managing three patients at once, handing critical work to aides, and watching good people get generic care.

He opened CPT in 2020 to do the opposite: full 60-minute sessions with a licensed therapist, hands-on manual work, and the time to understand every patient as a whole person, not a diagnosis code.

Courtney Crawford, PT, CIDN, joined with 17+ years of orthopedic experience — doubling our capacity without diluting the model. Megan Zombory and Brad Holtcamp followed, bringing additional depth in manual therapy and dry needling. Today, CPT serves patients from Geauga County, eastern Lake County, and the surrounding communities from our Fifth Avenue clinic.
