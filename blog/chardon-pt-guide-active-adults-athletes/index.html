Whether you're dealing with a sports injury, chronic pain, or simply looking to enhance your athletic performance, Chardon Performance Therapy is here to help you get back to doing what you love most.

We understand that the road to recovery can be challenging, but with the right support and expertise, you can overcome any obstacle. Our clinic is designed to be a welcoming and supportive environment where you can focus on your health and wellness.

At Chardon Performance Therapy, we prioritize your well-being above all else, working tirelessly to ensure that you receive the highest quality care. Specialized Care for Every Need

At Chardon Performance Therapy, we understand that each client has unique needs and goals. This is why we specialize in three key areas: Golf Performance, Running Performance, and Life Performance. These specializations allow us to offer targeted treatments that address the specific physical demands of each activity, ensuring that you receive the care you need to succeed. Whether you’re looking to improve your golf game, enhance your running form, or simply regain the ability to perform daily tasks without pain, our team of experts is here to help.

Our specialization in Golf Performance focuses on enhancing the physical skills necessary to excel in golf. This includes improving flexibility, strength, and coordination, all of which are crucial for a powerful and accurate swing.

Similarly, our Running Performance program is designed to help runners of all levels improve their biomechanics, prevent injuries, and achieve their best performance.

Finally, our Life Performance therapy is tailored to help you perform better in your everyday life, whether you’re recovering from surgery, managing a chronic condition, or simply looking to improve your overall physical health.

Each of these specializations is designed to help you reach your full potential, both on and off the field.

Golf Performance: Elevating Your Game

Golf is a sport that demands a high level of physical fitness and precision. Our Golf Performance program is designed to help you elevate your game by focusing on the key physical components of golf—flexibility, strength, and coordination.

Whether you're a seasoned golfer or just starting, our therapists work with you to develop a customized program that addresses your specific needs and goals. By incorporating manual therapy, strength training, and targeted exercises, we help you improve your swing, prevent injuries, and play at your best.

In addition to improving your performance on the course, our Golf Performance program also focuses on injury prevention. Golfers are prone to specific injuries, such as lower back pain and shoulder strains, which can significantly impact their ability to play.

Our therapists are trained to identify and address these issues early on, helping you avoid long-term damage and stay in the game. Running Performance: Achieving Your Best Stride

Running is a physically demanding activity that requires proper form, strength, and endurance. At Chardon, our Running Performance program is designed to help runners of all levels achieve their best stride. Whether you’re training for a marathon or enjoy a daily jog, our therapists assess your running form, identify any imbalances or weaknesses, and create a customized plan to address these issues.

By focusing on biomechanics, strength training, and injury prevention, we help you run more efficiently and reduce the risk of injury.

Our Running Performance program is not just about improving your speed or distance; it's about helping you run smarter and safer. Many common running injuries, such as shin splints and IT band syndrome, are often the result of improper form or muscle imbalances. Our therapists work with you to correct these issues, ensuring that you can run pain-free and with confidence. Our Chardon Physical Therapy team believes that every runner deserves to enjoy their sport without the fear of injury, and we are here to help you achieve that.

Life Performance: Enhancing Everyday Function

Life Performance therapy at Chardon PT is all about improving your ability to perform daily activities with ease and without pain. Whether you’re recovering from a work-related injury, dealing with chronic pain, or simply looking to enhance your overall physical health, our therapists are here to help. We take a holistic approach to your care, focusing on improving strength, mobility, and flexibility, so you can enjoy your life to the fullest.

Our Life Performance program is designed to help you regain control over your body and your life. This includes helping you return to activities you may have had to give up due to pain or injury. Whether that’s lifting your children, completing a day’s work without discomfort, or simply enjoying a walk in the park, our therapists work with you to develop a treatment plan that addresses your specific needs and goals.

Everyone deserves to live a life free from pain, and our Chardon Physical Therapy team is here to help you do that.

Comprehensive Treatment for Common Conditions

At Chardon PT, we treat a wide range of conditions that affect various parts of the body. Our therapists are experts in diagnosing and treating conditions such as back pain, shoulder injuries, knee pain, and ankle and foot injuries. We understand that each injury is unique, and we take a personalized approach to your care, developing a treatment plan that addresses your specific needs and goals.

Back pain and sciatica are two of the most common conditions we treat when it comes to Chardon Physical Therapy. These conditions can be caused by a variety of factors, including poor posture, heavy lifting, or sports injuries.

Our therapists use a combination of manual therapy, exercises, and education to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and restore mobility. We work with you to develop a treatment plan that not only addresses your current pain but also helps prevent future issues, ensuring that you can return to your daily activities without fear of reinjury.

The Advantages ofSelf-Pay Physical Therapy

One of the unique features of our Chardon Physical Therapy clinic is our self-pay option, which offers a range of benefits that can enhance your therapy experience. With self-pay, you have greater flexibility and control over your treatment plan. This means that you can choose when and how often you want to attend therapy sessions, allowing for more consistent care without the constraints of insurance approvals. This flexibility ensures that your therapy plan fits into your schedule and meets your needs.

In addition to the flexibility and control that self-pay offers, it also allows for more personalized and comprehensive care. Without the limitations imposed by insurance companies, our therapists can focus entirely on your individual needs, tailoring each session to your specific goals and progress. This means that you receive more effective care that is aligned with your personal health objectives.

Furthermore, self-pay eliminates the hassle of dealing with insurance claims, approvals, and denials, allowing you to start your therapy immediately and continue without interruption. With transparent and predictable costs, self-pay physical therapy makes it easier for you to budget for your care and make informed decisions about your treatment.

Education and Prevention: Empowering You for Long-Term Success

Education is a critical component of successful any Chardon Physical Therapy plan. We not only treat your current injuries but also empower you with the knowledge and tools you need to prevent future injuries and maintain a high level of physical health. Our therapists work closely with you to develop a deeper understanding of your body, teaching you proper techniques for exercise, stretching, and daily activities. This proactive approach ensures that you are equipped to take control of your health and prevent the recurrence of injuries.

Prevention is a key focus at Chardon Performance Therapy. We believe that by educating our clients about the importance of proper body mechanics, stretching, and strengthening exercises, we can help them avoid future injuries and maintain a pain-free lifestyle.

Our therapists are committed to providing you with the resources and support you need to succeed in your recovery and beyond. Whether you’re an athlete looking to stay in peak condition or someone recovering from an injury, we are here to help you achieve long-term success.
