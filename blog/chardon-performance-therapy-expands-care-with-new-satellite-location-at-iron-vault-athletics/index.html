Chardon Performance Therapy Expands Care with New Satellite Location at Iron Vault Athletics

By Chardon PT Team · March 12, 2026

At Chardon Performance Therapy, the goal has always been simple: help active people stay active. Whether that means training harder, recovering smarter, or finally fixing the issue that has been holding you back — our focus is helping you move with confidence again.

We're excited to announce a new satellite care location in partnership with Iron Vault Athletics. This partnership lets us bring the same high-level performance therapy experience to more people in the community — without compromising the individualized care our clients expect.

Why this partnership matters

Many of the people we work with are athletes, runners, parents, and active professionals who simply want their bodies to keep up with their lives. Expanding into Iron Vault Athletics lets us meet those individuals where they already train and move.

This satellite location helps us:

  • Increase access to care for active individuals in the area
  • Maintain the same one-on-one performance therapy model
  • Provide a convenient option for athletes who already train at Iron Vault
  • Continue delivering focused, individualized treatment plans

The goal isn't to change how we care for clients — it's to make that care easier to access.

Meet Courtney — PT & Running Specialist

Our clinician Courtney Crawford, PT — a certified running gait analyst and movement specialist — is providing care at the Iron Vault location every Friday.

Courtney works with runners, field athletes, and active adults who want to:

  • Reduce injury risk
  • Improve running mechanics
  • Return to sport after injury
  • Build strength that actually supports performance

For runners, in particular, having a clinic inside a training facility is a unique advantage. It means we can assess movement, strength, and mechanics in a real-world performance setting — not on a treatment table in a quiet room.

Same quality care — just another location

Adding a satellite location does not change how we deliver care. Clients can still expect:

  • A full conversation about what's really going on with your body
  • Clear explanations in simple language — no jargon, no hand-waving
  • A customized care plan built around your goals
  • Hands-on treatment combined with performance-focused strength work

Our mission hasn't shifted: help people get back to training, competing, working, and showing up fully in their life — without worrying their body is going to fail them.

Scheduling is easy

Booking an appointment at the Iron Vault satellite location is just as simple as scheduling at our primary clinic. If Fridays work best for your schedule, or if you already train at Iron Vault Athletics, this new location may be a great fit.

Contact Chardon Performance Therapy today to schedule your initial assessment and see if the Iron Vault location is right for you.

Your body deserves a plan that works — and a team that'll help you follow it through.
