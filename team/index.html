Meet

Dave Nelson, PT, DPT, CIDN

Dave founded Chardon Performance Therapy in 2020 to practice the way he believed PT should be practiced: one therapist, one patient, a full hour. No shortcuts, no aides running drills, no carousel of three people on a table at the same time.

His clinical focus spans complex orthopedic cases, sports rehab, and golf performance. He's the only TPI Medical Level 2 certified PT in the Chardon area — which means he evaluates how your body moves and how your swing moves, then builds the plan around both.

Outside the clinic, Dave is a husband, father, and dedicated athlete himself. He understands firsthand how an injury can upend everything you care about — and how the right plan can get you back.

Clinical focus

Complex orthopedic conditions (back, neck, shoulder, knee, hip)
Sports injury rehab & return-to-play
Golf-specific screening, biomechanics, and performance (TPI)
Dry needling for chronic pain and movement restriction
Post-operative rehab & blood flow restriction

Meet

Courtney Crawford, PT, CIDN

Courtney brings more than 17 years of outpatient orthopedic experience to CPT. She's worked across the spectrum — from post-op shoulders and knees to chronic low-back pain, from high-school athletes to retirees — and she builds trust quickly, explains the "why," and focuses on getting people back to life.

She earned her CIDN certification because she believes dry needling, used well, can unlock stubborn pain that stretching and strengthening alone won't touch. In her hands, it's another precision tool — not a gimmick.

Clinical focus

Outpatient orthopedic PT (17+ years)
Dry needling for neck, shoulder, and low back
Manual therapy & soft-tissue work
Post-surgical rehab (shoulder, knee, spine)
Patient education & long-term management

The Standard

Why It Matters Who You See

At most clinics, you start with a PT, then get passed to an aide, then maybe a different therapist next visit. At CPT, you keep your provider from day one through discharge.

Licensed PT, every minute

Your appointment is the full hour with a licensed doctor of physical therapy. Period.

Consistent provider

You see the same therapist at every visit, so progress is tracked and nothing gets lost.

Advanced certifications

CIDN, TPI Medical 2, BFR — we invest in continuing education so you get modern, evidence-informed care.

Open communication

Text us, call us, email us. Between visits we're still thinking about your case.
