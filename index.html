About Our Practice

Physical Therapy Built Around You

At Chardon Performance Therapy, every session is truly 1-on-1 with your therapist — no aides, no rotating staff, no being shared between patients. We take the time to understand your entire health picture and build a plan that actually fits your life.

Founded in 2020 by Dave Nelson, PT, DPT, CIDN, our practice was built on a belief that patients deserve better than the assembly-line PT model. Whether you're recovering from surgery, managing chronic pain, training for an event, or just want to move without limitations — we're here for you.

Your First Visit

What to Expect Before You Walk Through the Door

You should never be surprised by a PT appointment. Here's exactly how the first visit works.

1
Free phone consult
A quick call to understand what's going on, confirm we're the right fit, and walk you through cash pricing and HSA/FSA options. No pressure.
2
Full 60-minute evaluation
One therapist, one patient, the whole session. History, goals, hands-on assessment, movement screen, and a clear diagnosis.
3
Same-day treatment + a plan
You get hands-on treatment in visit one, a written plan for what's next, and a straight answer on what recovery actually looks like.
Full FAQ & Pricing Info

Cash-based & out-of-network

We do not bill insurance directly. You pay at the time of visit and we provide a superbill for potential out-of-network reimbursement. HSA/FSA accepted. Transparent pricing — call and we'll walk you through it.

Direct Access in Ohio

You don't need a referral to see a PT in Ohio. Schedule directly and we'll coordinate with your physician if needed.

Bring With You
