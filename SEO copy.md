Every page MUST follow:

## 1. HERO (ABOVE THE FOLD)
- H1 (primary keyword)
- Subheadline (value proposition)
- CTA (immediate)

## 2. QUICK ANSWER BLOCK (AI OPTIMIZATION)

## 3. PROBLEM SECTION
- What the user is experiencing

## 4. SOLUTION SECTION
- What you offer

## 5. BENEFITS SECTION
- Bullet points
- Clear outcomes

## 6. PROCESS SECTION
- Step-by-step

## 7. PROOF SECTION
- Testimonials / stats

## 8. COMPARISON / DECISION SECTION (CRITICAL)
- vs competitors
- vs alternatives

## 9. FAQ SECTION (AI GOLD)
- 4–8 questions

## 10. FINAL CTA
- Strong close
