About the Show

Why we started this.

Chardon is full of people quietly doing great work — small business owners who built something real, athletes chasing something bigger, clinicians who care deeply about their craft. Most of their stories never get told outside of a few tables at a coffee shop.

The Chardon Biz Podcast exists to fix that. Every episode is a real conversation — no script, no agenda — about what it actually takes to build and keep something good going in a small town.

If you run a business, coach a team, or just care about what's happening in Chardon, you'll get something out of it.

What you'll hear

Conversations with local business owners
Athletes & coaches on what drives them
Clinicians on their craft & patient care
Community builders on making Chardon better
Honest talks on movement, injury & performance
Be on the Show
