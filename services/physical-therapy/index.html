What Makes Our PT Different

Real Time. Real Plans. Real Results.

Most physical therapy clinics run an assembly line — a therapist managing 3–4 patients an hour with the actual hands-on work delegated to a tech. We don't.

Every session at Chardon Performance Therapy is a full 60 minutes with the same licensed PT, every visit. Manual therapy, dry needling, corrective exercise, and treatment progression are all done by your therapist — because we believe expertise shouldn't be optional.

It's the way PT was meant to work, and it's why patients keep telling us they finally feel heard.

What's included in every session

Full 60 minutes with your licensed PT — never split, never rushed.
Hands-on manual therapy — soft-tissue work, joint mobilization, dry needling when indicated.
Corrective exercise programming — built around your goals, not a generic worksheet.
Education — we teach you exactly what's wrong and how we're fixing it.
Home program — clear, photographed, and realistic for your schedule.
Progress tracking — measurable reassessments so you know it's working.

What to Expect

How Your First Few Visits Look

A clear, calm process — so you always know where you are in your recovery and what comes next.

New Patient Info Request Appointment
1
Free phone consult (optional)
15 minutes on the phone. We listen, ask questions, and make sure PT is the right fit before you commit to a visit.
2
Initial evaluation (60 min)
Full history, hands-on movement assessment, root-cause diagnosis, same-day treatment, and a clear plan you leave with.
3
Treatment sessions (60 min each)
Manual therapy, dry needling when indicated, progressive corrective exercise, and a home program that actually fits your week.
4
Reassess & progress
Every 3–4 visits we re-test and adjust the plan. You stay in control — we tell you the truth about what we see.
5
Discharge with a plan
When you're ready, we send you off with a maintenance program — and you're always welcome back if something flares.
