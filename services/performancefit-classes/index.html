Clinician-Programmed Classes

PerformanceFIT Classes in Chardon, OH

Small-group training led by clinicians. Four focused formats — Horse Power strength, Pilates, Running, and ParentStrong — built for adults who want to train smart, stay healthy, and keep doing what they love for decades.

Why PerformanceFIT

Training That Respects Your Body

We built PerformanceFIT because the gym model doesn't work for most adults. Big-box classes are too generic. Private training is too expensive. CrossFit-style intensity chews up joints and recurring injuries.

Our classes sit right in the middle — small groups (real attention), clinician programming (smart progressions), and a culture of training for the next 30 years, not Instagram. Whether you're finishing PT, returning after a baby, or just want to age well, there's a class for you.

And if something ever hurts, your instructor is a licensed clinician. We don't push through. We figure it out.

What you get in every class

Small group size — the coach actually knows you
Warm-up, main work, and cool-down — a full dose
Modifications built in — injuries are handled, not ignored
Progressive programming — you actually get stronger
A community of adults who care about moving well
The Four Formats

Pick the Format That Fits Your Goals

Horse Power

Our flagship strength class — barbells, dumbbells, kettlebells. Built for adults who want to get stronger without getting hurt.

  • Progressive strength
  • Rotary power for golfers
  • Smart modifications

Pilates

Mat-based Pilates programmed around spinal health, hip mobility, and deep-core control. Amazing for backs that don't love heavy lifting.

  • Spine-friendly core work
  • Mobility & control
  • Great for post-PT

Running

Gait-focused run programming with strength and mobility support. Couch-to-5K through marathon — we meet you where you are.

  • Gait & cadence work
  • Injury-prevention lifting
  • Beginner-friendly

ParentStrong

Specifically for postpartum parents and parents of young kids. Strength, core restoration, and the realities of lifting toddlers.

  • Postpartum-friendly
  • Pelvic-floor-aware
  • Energy you can use
