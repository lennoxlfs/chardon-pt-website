1-on-1 Physical Therapy

A full 60 minutes with your licensed therapist — no aides, no shared sessions. Hands-on treatment, custom programming, and a plan that actually works for your life.

  • Chronic pain & TMJ

Dry Needling (CIDN)

Evidence-based trigger-point therapy performed by two CIDN-certified clinicians. Fast relief for stubborn muscle pain, chronic tightness, and movement restrictions.

  • Both clinicians CIDN-certified
  • Headaches, neck, shoulder, back
  • Often paired with manual therapy
Learn more

Blood Flow Restriction

Advanced BFR training lets you build strength with light loads — ideal for post-op rehab, tendon pain, and returning from injury without overloading healing tissue.

  • Post-op protected loading
  • Tendon & overuse injuries
  • Clinician-supervised protocols
Learn more

TPI Golf Performance

Titleist Performance Institute–certified golf therapy. We connect swing faults to your body's mobility, stability, and strength — so pain goes down and distance goes up.

  • TPI screen + swing analysis
  • Body-to-swing correlation
  • Rare in Geauga County
Learn more

PerformanceFIT Classes

Small-group training led by clinicians. Horse Power strength, Pilates, Running, and ParentStrong — programmed for adults who want to train smart for life.

  • Horse Power & Pilates
  • Running & ParentStrong
  • Clinician-programmed
