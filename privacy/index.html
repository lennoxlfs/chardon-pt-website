Privacy Policy

How Chardon Performance Therapy collects, uses, and protects information through this website. Last updated April 2026.

This policy explains how Chardon Performance Therapy ("CPT," "we," "us," "our") handles information on our website (chardonperformancetherapy.com) and when you contact us through the site. This policy covers website interactions. Clinical records and protected health information (PHI) are governed by our separate HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices, which you'll receive at your first visit.

1. Information we collect

Information you give us

When you fill out our contact form, request an appointment, or email us, we collect the information you provide — typically your name, phone number, email address, and a description of what you're contacting us about. We treat that information with care and use it only to respond to your inquiry and coordinate care.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, we collect technical information when you visit — your IP address, browser type, device type, referring site, pages viewed, and approximate location. This is standard web traffic data and does not personally identify you.

2. Cookies and analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Vercel Analytics to understand how visitors find and use our site. These tools use cookies and similar technologies. They measure things like page views, referral sources, and which pages are most useful — not who you are as an individual. IP addresses are anonymized.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by using browser settings that block analytics cookies.

3. How we use information

  • To respond to your inquiry, schedule an appointment, or answer pre-visit questions
  • To send appointment reminders and care-related communications
  • To improve our website and understand how patients find us
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with advertisers or third-party marketers.

4. Sharing your information

We share information only with service providers that help us operate — like our website host (Vercel), analytics providers (Google Analytics), email tools, electronic medical records systems, and payment processors — and only for the specific purpose of providing you service. These vendors are bound by confidentiality and, where applicable, HIPAA Business Associate Agreements.

We may also disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect safety.

5. Data security

We use industry-standard safeguards — HTTPS encryption, access controls, and secure vendors — to protect information. Clinical records are stored in HIPAA-compliant systems. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect yours.

6. Retention

Website inquiry data is retained for the period necessary to respond to your inquiry and for legitimate business record-keeping. Clinical records are retained in accordance with Ohio law (typically 7 years past the last date of service for adults; longer for minors).

7. Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to legal limitations. To exercise these rights or for any privacy question, contact us at team@chardonpt.com or (440) 299-7171.

8. HIPAA and protected health information

Clinical information shared during your treatment is protected health information (PHI) and governed by HIPAA. You'll receive our Notice of Privacy Practices at your first appointment. Our website is not intended for transmission of PHI — please call or visit us to discuss clinical information securely.

9. Children's privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them online. If you're a parent or guardian and believe we've inadvertently collected information about your child, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Third-party links

Our website may link to external sites (social media, mapping tools, podcast platforms, continuing-education resources). Those sites have their own privacy policies, and we don't control or take responsibility for their practices.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated prominently on the website.
